# Gondwana / Terra -- Phase 1 Specification

## 1. Objective

Establish a satellite-driven ecological baseline across the 10 km
flagship zone. Deliver monthly ecological intelligence to support
structural optimisation and bushcare mobilisation.

## 2. Scope

• Monthly Sentinel-2 NDVI composite (cloud-masked)

• Structural complexity proxy (NDVI variance + Sentinel-1 where
feasible)

• Disturbance detection (fire/clearing)

• Connectivity & corridor gap mapping

• Plot-level regional ecosystem identification

• Mission recommendations (structural interventions)

## 3. Out of Scope (Phase 1)

• Credit issuance

• Portfolio-level verification

• Full acoustic coverage

• Elite classification

## 4. Deliverables

• Automated monthly ecological composite

• Interactive zone map interface

• Plot-level ecosystem preference output

• 3--5 structural missions initiated

• Stakeholder feedback loop established

## 5. Success Metrics

• Monthly pipeline operational

• Clear intelligence briefs generated

• Structural hotspot identification validated

• Active engagement from bushcare / catchment groups
